About Society for Conversion GIS Membership

If you are a GIS professional or are interested in using GIS for conservation, you can become a member of SCGIS!
The diverse membership includes members from many different countries in the world. Join this expanding network today!

Benefits

As an SCGIS member, you get access to a large and expanding network of conservation GIS professionals worlwide. And, you can meet many of your fellow members at the SCGIS annual conference in California!
When you become a member, you can:

  • Be part of a dynamic community of passionate GIS conservaniosts that motivate and support each other
    Engage in discussions, seek advice, and get GIS technical support from other members on our website and listserv
  • Access years of knowledge fom the past listserv email conversations via our lstserv archive
    Get discounted registration for the SCGIS Annual Conference
  • Attend free webinars offered to share technical and conservation knowledge with SCGIS members around the world
  • Create a member profile, browse other member profiles, and send private messages to members
  • Post descriptions of the GIS projects that you are working on
  • Create or join an SCGIS chapter
  • Browse and post GIS-related jobs on the jobs board Post GIS-related news and events
